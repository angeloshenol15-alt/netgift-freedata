How to actually spot this

  • 1 If a "free offer" has a countdown timer, slow down — urgency is the tactic, not the deadline.
  • 2 Check the actual web address. Real offers come from a company's real domain, not a random link shared in a group chat.
  • 3 Never enter your phone number, OTP, or card details to "claim" something you didn't request.
  • 4 If you're unsure, go to the company's official app or website directly and check there — don't follow the link.
  • 5 Tell the person who shared it with you. Most people share these because they got fooled too, not because they meant harm.
